Job Title: Task Force Hotel Operations Manager
Company: Pyramid Global Hospitality
Location: Not specified (travel required)
Job ID: R0062807
Job Type: Full time, Temporary (Task Force)

Job Description:
We are seeking a highly motivated, adaptable, and hands-on Task Force Hotel Operations Manager with strong experience in overseeing hotel or resort operations. This role serves as a temporary, high-impact leadership position, responsible for stabilizing, supporting, and improving hotel operations during transitions, openings, performance challenges, or leadership gaps.

Your Role:
- Assume interim operational leadership for assigned hotel or resort properties as needed
- Oversee daily hotel operations, ensuring brand standards, policies, and procedures are consistently met
- Lead and support department heads across Rooms, Front Office, Housekeeping, Maintenance, Food & Beverage (if applicable), and Guest Services
- Drive guest satisfaction and service excellence throughout the entire guest experience
- Monitor and manage labor productivity, scheduling, and payroll to align with budgetary goals
- Review financial performance, forecasts, and expense controls; identify opportunities to improve profitability
- Assess operational challenges quickly and implement corrective action plans
- Coach, mentor, and support hotel leadership teams; provide hands-on training as required
- Ensure smooth transitions during openings, leadership changes, renovations, or ownership changes
- Partner with Sales, Revenue Management, and Corporate teams to support occupancy, ADR, and service initiatives
- Ensure standards for safety, cleanliness, maintenance, and regulatory compliance are met
- Communicate effectively with ownership, leadership teams, and corporate stakeholders
- Provide detailed handover documentation and operational recaps at the completion of each assignment
- Stay informed of hotel industry trends and recommend operational improvements where applicable

Qualifications:
- Proven hotel operations leadership experience (Assistant General Manager, General Manager, or equivalent)
- Strong understanding of hotel financials, labor management, and operational KPIs
- Ability to quickly adapt to new properties, brands, systems, and team cultures
- Excellent communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills
- Hands-on, visible leadership style with a strong service mindset
- Willingness and ability to travel on short notice for temporary assignments
- Experience working in task force, interim, or multi-property environments preferred

Employment Status & Benefits:
Task Force roles are classified as temporary, full-time positions. Temporary employees are not eligible for medical, dental, life insurance, paid time off, retirement savings plans, or bonus programs.

Compensation:
Task Force assignments are compensated on a weekly rate basis and paid biweekly. Compensation is determined based on role level, scope, location, and assignment complexity. The pay range for this position is $1,500–$2,500 per week, depending on assignment level. Actual compensation will be confirmed at the time of assignment.
